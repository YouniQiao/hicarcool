# Switching Audio Playback Devices

After successfully connecting to HiCar, audio is switched to the vehicle's speakers for playback by default. You can switch the audio playback device between your phone (including headphones connected to the phone, etc.) and the vehicle's audio system as needed.

## Quick Switch via Phone Notification Bar

1. After successfully connecting to HiCar, swipe down from the top of the phone screen to open the notification center.

2. Find **HiCar Smart Connectivity** in the notification bar, tap the downward arrow on the right to expand more features.

    <img
        src={require('./img/switch-audio-device-1.png').default}
        width="400" 
    />

    Starting from version 14.2.0.146, the banner notification has been optimized as shown below:

    <img
        src={require('./img/switch-audio-device-4.jpg').default}
        width="400" 
    />

3. Tap the **Switch Audio to Phone** text or button (the second button on the right) to switch calls, music, navigation voice, etc., to the phone for playback.

    After switching to the phone, if the phone is also connected to headphones, smart glasses, or other devices, audio will automatically play through those devices.

    After switching to the phone, the button name will change to **Switch Audio to [Vehicle Model Name]** . Tapping it again will switch the audio back to the head unit for playback.

## Quick Switch During a Call

After connecting to HiCar, you can switch a call back to the phone or other devices connected to the phone (such as headphones, smart glasses, etc.) during a call.

1. On the call interface, tap the third button from left to right to enter the device selection list.

    <img
        src={require('./img/switch-audio-device-2.png').default}
        width="800" 
    />

2. In the device selection list, choose the desired output device for the call.
