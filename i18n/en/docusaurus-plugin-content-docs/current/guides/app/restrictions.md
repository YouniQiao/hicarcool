# App Usage Restrictions

HiCar strictly restricts apps that may affect driving safety. You cannot open apps marked with a **"P"** badge in the lower-right corner of their icon while the vehicle is in motion; you can only use such apps when the vehicle is parked.

<img
    src={require('./img/restrictions-1.png').default}
    width="400" 
/>

These apps appear grayed out and cannot be tapped while driving.

<img
    src={require('./img/restrictions-2.png').default}
    width="400" 
/>

:::tip
Some vehicle models or devices may not correctly report the driving status, which could allow these apps to still be usable while driving. It is recommended that you close such apps while driving and only use them when parked.
:::
