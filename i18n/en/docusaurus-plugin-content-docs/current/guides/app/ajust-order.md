# Adjusting App Order

You can freely adjust the display order of apps on the app icon page after connecting to HiCar according to your preference.

1. Depending on whether HiCar is connected, choose a different method to enter the app management interface.
    * When HiCar is connected, swipe down from the top of the phone screen to open the notification center, and tap the HiCar Smart Connectivity banner notification in the notification bar.
    * When HiCar is not connected, go to your phone's **Settings > More Connections > HiCar Smart Connectivity**, then tap the HiCar-connected device under **My Devices**.

2. Tap **HiCar Smart Connectivity App Management**.

    <img
        src={require('./img/obtain-2.jpg').default}
        width="400" 
    />

3. Press and hold an icon and drag it to adjust the app display order.
