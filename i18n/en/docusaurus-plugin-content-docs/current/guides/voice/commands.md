# Common Voice Commands

<img
    src={require('./img/commands-1.png').default}
    width="800" 
/>

After successfully connecting to HiCar, you can enter the **Play Tips** app to view detailed voice commands.

You can use voice commands for driving navigation, media/music, voice games, and more. Common voice commands include:

* **Driving Navigation:** "Navigate home", "Navigate to work", "Navigate to the nearest convenience store", "Navigate to the nearest gas station", etc.
* **Media Music:** "Play music", "Play a song", "Play White Dragon Horse", "Play piano music", "Play songs by Andy Lau", "Favorite", "Next track", "Unfavorite", "Pause", "Resume", "Loop playback", etc.
* **Audio Radio:** "Next episode", "Resume playback", "Pause playback", etc.
* **Communication & Calls:** "Call 122", "Call Dad", "I want to call Mom", "Call Huawei customer service", "Help me call China Merchants Bank", etc.
* **Daily Convenience:** "What's the date today?", "What's the weather today?", "What time is it now?", etc.
* **Voice Games:** "Poetry chain", "Riddles", "I want to play idiom chain", etc.
