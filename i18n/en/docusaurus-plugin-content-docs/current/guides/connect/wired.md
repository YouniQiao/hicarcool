# Wired Connection

Some phones or head units, especially after-market head units, can only connect to HiCar via a wired connection due to hardware limitations.

## Prerequisites

1. Your phone supports HiCar. Only a few phones do not support HiCar wireless connection and only support wired connection. You can check phone support here: [HiCar Supported Phones](https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/development/HiCar-Guides/available-phones-0000001227954439).

2. Your car model supports HiCar. Only a few original equipment and after-market head units support only wired connection.
   * For original equipment and after-market head unit support, you can check here: [HiCar Supported Models](https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/development/HiCar-Guides/available-models-0000001226230373).
   * For adapter box support, you can check the product sales detail page. Currently, almost all adapter box solutions on the market support both wired and wireless connection to HiCar.

## Steps

1. Use a USB data cable to connect your phone to the head unit. On the first connection, follow the prompts on your phone screen to complete the wired connection.

    :::tip

    Please use official Huawei phone cables to connect to HiCar to avoid connection failures or instability caused by cable issues.

    :::

2. Tap "Connect" in the popup.
