# Entering Driving Mode

After updating to a HiCar version that supports Driving Mode, there are two ways to enter: first-time entry and subsequent automatic entry.

## First-Time Entry

The first-time entry to Driving Mode is simple. After updating to a HiCar version with Driving Mode, follow these steps:

Settings > More Connections > HiCar Smart Travel > Tap the four dots icon in the top right corner > Create desktop shortcut.

<img
  src={require('./img/enter-driving-mode-1.jpg').default}
  width="400" 
/>

After this operation, you will see the "Driving Mode" icon on your desktop. Tap the icon to enter Driving Mode.

## Automatic Entry

The Driving Mode settings provide an option to automatically start when connected to the vehicle's Bluetooth. The steps are as follows:

1. Enter Driving Mode using the method described above.

2. Tap the first icon on the left side of the bottom Dock to switch to the "More Apps" list.
   
   <img
    src={require('./img/enter-driving-mode-2.jpg').default}
    width="400" 
   />

3. Tap "Settings", enter "Auto-start on Bluetooth Connection", turn on "Auto-start HiCar Smart Travel", select the vehicle Bluetooth, and then tap "Enable".
   
   <img
    src={require('./img/enter-driving-mode-3.jpg').default}
    width="400" 
   />
   
   From then on, when you get in your vehicle and your phone automatically connects to the vehicle Bluetooth, Driving Mode will start automatically.
