# Screenshot

## Voice Screenshot

Starting from HiCar App version 13.2.0.506, you can take a screenshot directly by voice. After connecting to HiCar and waking up the voice assistant, simply say "Screenshot". The screenshot image is saved to your phone's photo album.

## Manual Screenshot

The HiCar screenshot feature relies on the Driving Mode function on your phone. If you are not familiar with HiCar Driving Mode, you can click [Driving Mode Introduction](../../dmode/driving-mode.md) to learn more.

When you are connected to HiCar and turn on the Driving Mode function on your phone, the Driving Mode interface will change to the HiCar remote control style shown below. At this point, tap the "Screenshot" button in the center to take a screenshot of the HiCar interface. The captured image is saved to your phone's photo album.
