# Desktop Cards

Desktop cards are a quick operation method provided by Next HiCar. You can quickly learn about app/service information, enter apps/services, and perform shortcuts provided by apps/services through these cards.

## Card Categories

<img
    src={require('./img/desktop-1.jpg').default}
    width="800" 
/>

Cards mainly include the following categories:
* Map Card: Displays map app shortcuts, such as "Go Home", "Go to Company", and navigation summary information during navigation.
* Audio Card: Displays audio app shortcuts, including previous track, play/pause, and next track.
* Calendar Card: Displays the day's schedule information from the calendar app.
* Weather Card: Displays weather information for your current location from the weather app.
* Smart Recommendation Card: Displays app recommendations, tips, and other information.


:::tip

Cards are automatically generated and sorted by the system, requiring no manual intervention. This also means you cannot manually adjust the display order of cards.
:::


## Card Editing

Long-press a desktop card to enter the card editing page. You can quickly edit cards by tapping the add and delete buttons in the upper right corner of the card.
