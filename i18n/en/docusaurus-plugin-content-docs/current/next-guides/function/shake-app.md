# Shake to Transfer Apps

Next HiCar supports shaking your phone to transfer apps from your phone to the vehicle screen.

## Setup Methods

You can enable Shake to Transfer Navigation using any of the following methods:

* Go to your phone's "Settings > Multi-Device Collaboration > HUAWEI HiCar", tap the connected device under "My Devices", and turn on the Shake to Transfer Apps toggle.
  
    <img
        src={require('./img/shake-app-1.jpg').default}
        width="400" 
    />

* After connecting to HiCar, go to "Settings > Shake to Transfer Navigation" on the vehicle side and turn on the Shake to Transfer Apps toggle.

## Steps

1. Open an app that supports Shake to Transfer Apps on your phone. The live window on your phone will display the text "Can Transfer".
   
    <img
        src={require('./img/shake-app-2.jpg').default}
        width="400" 
    />

    A "Transfer" button will also appear in the banner notification.

    <img
        src={require('./img/shake-app-3.jpg').default}
        width="400" 
    />

2. Shake your phone, and the corresponding app will automatically open on the Next HiCar vehicle screen.
