# Wired Connection
Some phones or vehicle head units, especially aftermarket ones, can only connect to Next HiCar via a wired connection due to hardware limitations.

## Prerequisites

1. Your phone supports Next HiCar. Only a small number of phones do not support Next HiCar wireless connections and only support wired connections. You can check phone compatibility here: [HiCar Supported Phones](https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/development/HiCar-Guides/available-phones-0000001227954439).

2. Your vehicle model supports HiCar. Only a few original and aftermarket head units only support wired connections.
   * For original and aftermarket head unit compatibility, you can check here: [HiCar Supported Models](https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/development/HiCar-Guides/available-models-0000001226230373).
   * For connectivity box solutions, you can check the product sales page. Currently, almost all box solutions support both wired and wireless connections to HiCar.


## Steps

1. Use a USB data cable to connect your phone to the vehicle head unit. On the first connection, follow the prompts on your phone screen to complete the wired connection.

    :::tip

    Please use official Huawei phone cables to connect to Next HiCar to avoid connection issues or instability caused by cable problems.

    :::

2. Select "Connect" in the pop-up.


3. When prompted "Allow auto-connect HiCar", it is recommended to select "Allow".
