# Adjusting App Order

You can freely adjust the display order of apps on the app icon page after connecting to Next HiCar, according to your preferences.

Unlike classic HiCar, in Next HiCar you can directly press and hold an app icon and drag it to adjust the display order — just like using apps on your phone.
