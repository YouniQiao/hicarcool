# See & Say

<img
    src={require('./img/seen-and-said-1.png').default}
    width="800" 
/>

HiCar supports See & Say — after waking up the smart voice assistant, you can speak the text or badge you see on the screen to execute the corresponding tap action. Using the image above as an example, you can:
* Say "Featured Recommendations," "Playlist Square," "Podcasts," or "My Music" to switch between different tabs.
* Say "Daily Recommendations," "Personal Radar," "Time Radar," "New Song Radar," or "Cloud Music Rap Chart" to switch between different lists.
* Say "1," "2," "3," "4," or "5" to switch between different lists.

:::tip

Some apps that do not support this feature may not be usable with See & Say.

If no badges appear on the screen after waking up the voice assistant, it is likely that See & Say is not supported on the current screen.

:::
