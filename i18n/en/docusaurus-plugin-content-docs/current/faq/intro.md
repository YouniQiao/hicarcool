# Frequently Asked Questions

The FAQ section mainly lists some problems you may encounter while using HiCar. These issues may not be covered in the previous user manual instructions, such as "Why can't audio be split?", "Why does HiCar show 'Cannot run' when installed on the phone?", "Why doesn't dual-instance work for maps?", etc.

These questions are organized by category as much as possible, such as app-related issues, connection-related issues, support-related issues, audio-related issues, etc.

Adding to the FAQ is a long process and will be done gradually. If you come across this page and have some questions about HiCar, feel free to follow the official WeChat account "HiCar情报站" (HiCar News Station), leave a message with your questions, and we will try to prioritize adding your questions to the FAQ.
