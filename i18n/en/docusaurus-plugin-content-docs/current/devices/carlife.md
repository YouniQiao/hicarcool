# Vehicle Supports Wired CarLife

## Applicable Scenarios

Applicable for vehicles that do not support HiCar natively but support Baidu CarLife. Since there are third-party conversion boxes available now, you can use the CarLife channel to enable HiCar on vehicles that originally did not support it.

## How to Check

After starting your vehicle, look for CarLife-related menus or buttons in your vehicle's infotainment system, usually found under connectivity or multimedia submenus.
