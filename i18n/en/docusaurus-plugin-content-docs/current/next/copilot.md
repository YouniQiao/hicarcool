# Wallpaper Desktop

HiCar NEXT offers a brand-new wallpaper desktop for larger screens, somewhat reminiscent of the intelligent cockpit of certain Huawei smart selection vehicles.

## Wallpaper Desktop

<img
  src={require('./img/copilot-1.jpg').default}
  width="800" 
/>

Key features of the wallpaper desktop:
* The system Dock area, i.e., the navigation icon area, is located at the bottom of the desktop.
* Various cards on the desktop are arranged horizontally at the bottom.

HiCar automatically determines whether to display the wallpaper desktop based on the screen's aspect ratio; manual adjustment to the wallpaper desktop style is not supported.

## Non-Wallpaper Desktop (Smart Desktop)

Of course, if the interface you see looks like the image below, it means your screen does not meet the requirements for displaying the wallpaper desktop.
