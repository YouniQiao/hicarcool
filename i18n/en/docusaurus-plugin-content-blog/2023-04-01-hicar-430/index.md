## HiCar 13.2.0.430 Official Release

<img
  src={require('./hicar-430-2.jpg').default}
  width="400" 
/>

Main updates:
1. Updated apps in [More Apps].
2. Added support for version upgrade detection on some head units.
3. The "Phone" icon position on the Dock can now display "Recent Apps".

Not that it matters much, though — because the 431 version came along right after and seems to be rolling out faster.

The 430 official version might still be pushed to different devices at different paces in app markets.

## HiCar 13.2.0.431 Version Is Also Here

Some of you may not have even seen the 430 version yet, but have already received the 431 version. What's going on? Well, if you look back at what we wrote before, you'll understand — this is HiCar's unique version numbering rhythm. Link here: [HiCar Version Description](https://hicar.cool/docs/version).
